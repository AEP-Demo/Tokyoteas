Steeped to Perfection

At Tokyo Teas, we offer a wide selection of directly traded and micro-farmed artisan teas direct from the plantations of Japan. In addition to single garden teas, we also have a large selection of organic herbs, herbal infusions, teapots and tea accessories. We welcome you to explore and experience the world of fine teas with us.
More than “farm-to-table”, we believe in “people-to-people”—at Tokyo Teas, you can learn about the farmers who grow your tea, as we translate your feedback into Japanese so the farmers can also learn about you. Join us in a life steeped in tea!

  • Details

  • Sencha Fukamushi is usually a deeper color than regular Sencha. This tea is a bright and vivid green. Generally speaking, as you go up the scale of quality, Sencha is brighter and more vivid in color. The best quality teas are the most bright and vivid.

    The tea leaves used to make Sencha Fukamushi Premium are harvested when the sprouts are small and soft and not fully-grown; when the tea tree has only three sprouts. So, when you brew this tea, the full potential or energy of the young tea sprouts is extracted into the cup.
